    • About Cyber Awareness

    Cyber awareness refers to the knowledge and understanding of various cybersecurity threats, practices, and tools designed to protect personal, organizational, and governmental data from cyberattacks. It’s about recognizing the importance of maintaining a safe and secure digital environment and knowing how to minimize risks that come with using the internet and digital systems.

    ‘Safe Click’ Drive Launched To Control Cyber Crimes In Madhya Pradesh

    With an objective to raise cyber awareness among people, Madhya Pradesh Police has launched state-wide special awareness drive ‘safe click’ on Saturday which will conclude on February 11 on the occasion of Safer Internet Day.
    Under the programme, police will raise awareness among internet users and bank customers not only in urban areas but even in remote rural areas of the state.
